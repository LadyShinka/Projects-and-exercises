Our Mission

Habitat for Humanity believes that every man, woman and child should have a decent, safe and affordable place to live. We build and repair houses all over the world using volunteer labor and donations.

Why We Build

There are nearly two billion people around the world who live in slum housing and more than 100 million are homeless. We provide shelter and housing assistance to help these families.
